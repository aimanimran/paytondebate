Overview

Policy debate involves teams of two advocating for or against a resolution that calls for policy action taken by the US federal government.
The "resolution" or overarching topic remains the same throughout the year.

National Topic (23-24):
        The United States federal government should
        substantially increase fiscal redistribution in the
        United States by adopting a federal jobs guarantee,
        expanding Social Security, and/or providing a basic
        income.
